About Us

From telecom roots to AI-driven products

Done Iletisim Bilgi Sistemleri ve Yayincilik San. ve Tic. A.S. was founded in Istanbul and spent its first decades building large-scale mobile solutions for operators like Turkcell, Turk Telekom and Vodafone — reaching over 100 million consumers in more than 10 countries.

Today our focus is our own product: WeBee, a modern guest experience platform for hotels. Done owns the WeBee IP and trademark, and is the parent of WeBee Technology Solutions Ltd. (UK). Alongside WeBee, we deliver AI and custom software development for selected clients.

  • Deloitte Technology Fast 50 & Fast 500 — fastest-growing company (2006, 2015, 2016)
  • EU Horizon 2020 funding & Seal of Excellence
  • Best software company & innovation awards — 30+ in total
  • Trusted by global hotel brands: Kempinski, Mövenpick, Radisson, Wyndham, Rixos
